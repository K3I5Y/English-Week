Meaning

English Language Week is an academic celebration dedicated to strengthening and
promoting the English language through educational, recreational, and cultural activities.
It is aimed at students of all levels with the goal
of improving their linguistic and cultural competence.

¿Why is it celebrated?

This week is celebrated to encourage the use of English in real-life and fun contexts,
motivating students to practice it creatively.
It also allows students to learn more about Anglo-Saxon culture, including its
music, art, cuisine, and customs.
